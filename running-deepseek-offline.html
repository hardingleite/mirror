So Much Market Uproar...I Had To Test It Too.
DeepSeek-R1 is a reasoning-focused model based on two open source foundational models:

  • Alibaba's Qwen LLM: 1.5B, 7B, 14B, and 32B parameters

  • Meta's Llama LLM: 8B and 70B parameters

DeepSeek has released "distilled" versions of R1 based on these two models which were fine-tuned on synthetic data generated by R1.

This innovative 'distilled' technique, which emphasizes specialization and downsizing, led to the development of high-performance Small Language Models (SLMs) while reducing model size without sacrificing capability.

So, to remove the mistery in the word "distilled", I went and searched for a clear and simple definition.Here it is: Distillation means it has been trained in a way to retain most of the capabilities of a larger model while being smaller and more efficient ("thanks chatGPT").

In fact, some of its SLMs rival OpenAI-o1 performance, especially in reasoning tasks.

As an aside, in addition to Alibaba's and Meta's, DeepSeek has developed and trained its own series:  7B, 13B, 34B, and 70B parameter models (using approx. 2 trillion tokens with a context length of 4096 tokens).


Running DeepSeek on My Laptop: Offline and Secure.

These are the (simple) steps you can also take to host and run an open-source LLM locally on your own computer.

After seeing how DeepSeek took the market by surprise, I was curious about it, so I chose to test version "deepseek-r1-distill-qwen-7b".

Step 1
Download and Install LM STUDIO:https://lmstudio.ai
It has a really cool and intuitive user interface.

Note1: You may instead, opt to download and use OLLAMA [ https://ollama.ai ]

Note2: Both LM Studio and Ollama serve as that runtime environment, providing a user-friendly interface to interact with the (raw) model.

Step 2
From within LM STUDIO, choose the model you wish to download as its base LLM model (you may download additional open-source ones later).As previously mentioned, I chose the "deepseek-r1-distill-qwen-7b" LLM. See pics below.

Step 3 Disconnect your PC from the Internet and then, start using the GenAI Chat interface of LM Studio.

Step 4 (Bonus: RAG) Be sure to add external sources of information such as PDFs and DOCs related to your research, core business or college paper: you'll be delighted to see a RAG (Retrieval-Augmented Generation) fine-tuned and integrated with your GenAI Chat capabilities. No retraining of your LLM model needed.
