GraceAI is a workflow-based AI Agent system designed to create a complete 'call center in a box' solution.

This is a simulation of what a Conversational AI tool called GRACE AI can do: its capabilities, pros and cons and a mini step-by-step on how to implement a simple call-center-in-a-box in 2 hours.

Here are highlights of some of GRACE's available features.

Web-as-a-LLM (FAQs, How-to)

The system can be fed with text-centric web content (e.g., FAQs, How-To) hosted in your  Intranet or public web.



Edit the KB's Source Files

AI (and their LLMs) are excellent at Data Summarization. The Knowledge Base app allows you to fine-tune and edit your text-based datasets (e.g. scripts, guides, streams of chat, news, transcripts, emails). 



Control the (re)indexing of Docs

The system can be fed with text-centric web content (e.g., FAQs, How-To) hosted in your  Intranet or public web.



Setting up the architecture

As one of the first steps in getting the system up and running, the setup of the high-level components of the workflow enables system resources such as a pool of phone numbers available, native and 3rd party integrations,  logic of the workflow, among many other no-code conversational design elements.



Rich Business Logic Interface

The possibilities are vast within this no-code, drag-and-drop interface, being as intuitive as it gets. 

There are drop-down menus, diamonds for 'if-then-else' conditional logic, calls to APIs (e.g., checking time or meeting room availability), in-line JSON capture/eval, passing of variables ( tag names), etc.



Call Logging and Flow inspection

One of the most impressive features of the system is the ability to debug the flow in real-time in a closed-loop (i.e. not calling out an external phone number), inspecting and adjusting the iteration and business logic.


Free Native integrations

The AI ecosystem needs to extend its functionalities and capabilities to other systems, databases, calendars, ERPs, CRMs, etc

GRACE AI comes with a plethora of native--and free-- connectors for Salesforce, email, https, sms, Google Calendars, to mention just a few.



native Salesforce Integration

Tons of native (a.k.a. free) integrations with databases, Salesforce, email, HTTP requests, SMS etc



Adjust the Rep's personality

The design of the Conversational Rep's personality offers several categories and settings: 'Efficient', 'Fluid', 'Casual', 'Professional', 'Succinct'. On the video demo above, Grace was programmed to be very efficient and repeat what the caller has just said, for accuracy purposes.
